About Royal Rugs

Premium Quality Carpets, Crafted with Excellence

Company Profile

Royal Rugs Indonesia was established in 2020 as a premium local carpet manufacturer. With our own factory equipped with Zimmer tufting machines from Austria and other advanced machinery, we produce high-quality carpets using carefully selected yarns.

To date, we have successfully sold over 15,000 pieces of carpets both online and offline, with various patterns and sizes, reaching all corners of Indonesia. Our product quality and customer trust drive us to continue growing, making Royal Rugs part of the global market.

Our Vision

  • To become a leading and trusted carpet company in both domestic and international markets, known for quality products at affordable prices, accessible to all segments of society.
  • To be the most comprehensive and best carpet solution provider for various needs, whether residential, commercial, or industrial.
  • To contribute positively to the environment through sustainable and responsible business practices.
  • To become a superstore in the carpet industry by offering products that meet consumer needs in terms of price, size variety, and attractive designs.

  • Produce high-quality carpet products using the best raw materials and latest advanced technology.
  • Provide responsive, professional, and personalized customer service.
  • Continuously innovate in design, materials, and technology to meet evolving market needs.
  • Build long-term, mutually beneficial relationships with customers and business partners.
